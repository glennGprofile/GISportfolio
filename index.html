A small selection of maps made during my academic career. Click each thumbnail to be sent to the web map.

Map of potential sites to store contaminents in Cedar Area South and the risk they pose of polluting both local surface water and groundwater.

Mapping the theoretical path of a new water main in Nanaimo, attaching two connecting valves. Best path calculated based on cost, which considers both material costs and average cost of developing within each zone type.

Map of unharvestable areas in VIU Woodlot. Considerations include viewshed from Rutherford Mall, ground slope angle, and riparian zones.

This map was created by comparing accurate elevation data from Vancouver Island University to data collected through Avenza, on a phone, using satellite-based position information. Each dot represents a collected data point, with information about the recorded elevation, actual elevation, difference between the recorded and actual elevation, and a brief description of general features in the area when the location was recorded.
